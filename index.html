Prototyping Your Idea

Do you have a fantastic business idea, but need help getting it up and running? This workshop is aimed at helping potential founders get prototype websites up. With a prototype website, you can run ads, test conversion, and potentially estimate revenue without having to take on the risk of building it.

If your experiments work, you will have a compelling case to show to investors or potential hires to help make your dream a reality.

If your experiments don't work, you'll be able to figure out what went wrong and fix them before investing substantial resources.

Try hard, fail fast.
