Prototyping Your Idea

Do you have a fantastic business idea, but need help getting it up and running? This workshop is aimed at helping future founders get prototype websites up. With a prototype website, you can run ads, test conversion, and estimate revenue without having to take on the risk of fully building it out.

Assuming your experiments are successful, you will have metrics to show investors as well as a viable product to help convince future hires to make your dream a reality.

If your experiments do not perform as expected, you will have the necessary tools to iterate on your idea and implement fixes, all before investing substantial resources.

Try hard, fail fast.
